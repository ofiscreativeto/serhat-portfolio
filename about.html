Serhat Dogantekin

Director / Producer

Serhat is a Turkish-Canadian director and producer with a diverse background in film and advertising. With a proven track record of successfully managing simultaneous projects, coordinating large crews, and overseeing all aspects from pre-production to post-production, Serhat has demonstrated exceptional skills in the film and advertising industry. He has collaborated on successful projects for international clients and played a key role in the success of a nationally broadcasted TV series as a scriptwriter.
